My waycast, declaring my way

This is my waycast. It is the formal declaration of my way and my commitment to act on it. It gives the answers to these questions:

  • Where am I going?
  • What steps will take me there?
  • What step do I work on today?
  • Who works with me?

I think that in order to have a future, we must make it. I think of our future as a practical task in engineering, a thing to be built by design.
