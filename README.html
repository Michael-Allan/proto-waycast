My waycast, declaring my way

This is my waycast. It answers these questions:

  • Where am I going?
  • What steps will take me there?
  • What step am I on?
  • Who am I working with?

Throughout it shows the ways explored by other waycasters, too — the answers they have discovered and chosen for themselves — juxtaposed for comparison and contrast with mine. This makes the waycast a useful tool not only to declare a way, but to fashion one in the first place, even from scratch. All of which fits its wider disseminative role as the propagule and building block of a general, self instituting system of personal way formation.

Back to the topic with this: my own way, which you see cast here, begins with using my waycast in its disseminative role to seed the realization of that system, or of others like it.
