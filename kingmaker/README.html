Plugging myself into an executive power structure

Here I formally choose my leaders and peers in action. I place myself within a de facto, volunteer power structure — an organization of volunteers — thereby offering my support to others in a bid for their support in return. I think this will help me advance toward my goal.

Further, it invites candidates for elected office to bid for my electoral vote — to formally pledge their help once elected — by immediately plugging themselves into the same, volunteer power structure.
